Why get an abacus?

Do you need to upgrade your counting capabilities? Do you need to count in bases other than base 10? Than the abacus is here to help you. Estitmated to be used by ancient civilization since about 400 B.C., the abacus is an upgrade to the digits on the hands used by most people for counting. Here at Abacus-Abra we strive to bring you the greatest abacus devices using the best modern techniques and materials.
